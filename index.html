Beginner Friendly

Morning Light & Circadian Reset

Getting natural sunlight within 30–60 minutes of waking anchors your circadian rhythm, boosts cortisol at the right time, and improves sleep quality 8–12 hours later.

  • 1
    Go outside within 30 min of waking (no sunglasses)
  • 2
    Aim for 5–10 min on overcast days, 2–3 min on bright days
  • 3
    Combine with a short walk for compounding benefit
Moderate
🧊

Cold Exposure Protocol

Deliberate cold therapy activates brown adipose tissue, boosts norepinephrine up to 300%, and builds mental resilience through controlled stress adaptation.

  • 1
    Start with 30-sec cold showers, work up to 2–3 min
  • 2
    Cold water immersion (11°C / 52°F) is most effective
  • 3
    11 min/week total is enough for metabolic benefits
Advanced
🫁

Breathwork: CO₂ Tolerance Training

Improving CO₂ tolerance through breath-hold and box breathing exercises enhances oxygen delivery, reduces anxiety, and can lower resting heart rate over time.

  • 1
    Morning: 4-7-8 breathing (4 cycles) to activate parasympathetic
  • 2
    Box breathing 5.5 sec in / hold / out / hold — 5 min daily
  • 3
    Track BOLT score weekly to monitor CO₂ tolerance progress
Moderate
💤

Sleep Optimization Stack

Sleep is the highest-leverage biohack. A consistent protocol around temperature, light, and timing can dramatically improve deep sleep and REM quality.

  • 1
    Drop bedroom to 18–19°C (65–67°F) for sleep onset
  • 2
    Block blue light 2 hrs before bed or use red/dim lighting
  • 3
    Magnesium glycinate + L-theanine 30 min before bed
